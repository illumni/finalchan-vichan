  Misc. Rules (Warnings or guidelines rather)
  • Bumping a post with "bump" is unnecessary. We're a slow imageboard. If you want to bump a thread, actually contribute.
  • Posts that are off-topic for the board they are respectively posted in will be moved to the board better suited for the thread (do not take this as an insult, it just might fit another board better)
  • If you'd like to make a board suggestion, please make a thread on /r/ about it (i.e, "/sci/ Thread!", etc), if it gets enough attention, we might consider making a board dedicated to the subject of the thread.
  • Trips aren't really necessary outside of /sp/, so please keep them contained there unless there's a reason for it being outside of /sp/

If you have any questions, please view the FAQ.
