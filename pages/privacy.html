Finalchan Privacy Policy v1.0

Finalchan as a whole respects your privacy, this means that now and even as we might (or might not) grow larger in the near or distant future, we WILL NOT distribute, sell, exploit your information for the use of third parties. We DO NOT have any biased obligation to work with authorities, and will not release information, unless a situation arises where we absolutely must. Now I'll explain what information we do get.

The information we collect:

  • IP Addresses
  • Browser Information
  • Page Views
  • View Times
  • Your Operating System

Now, remember, this DOES NOT include information that YOU provide us with when (and if) you decide to post. If you choose to put an email in the Email Field of the post form, everyone that views the site will now have your email. We are not liable for any information you happen to leak about yourself through a post.

We use an analytics software that isn't shared or hosted by any third party. We use this information to help guide us through troubleshooting, enhancing Finalchan, and seeing what parts of Finalchan are doing good. Tinyboard, the site's imageboard software, collects IP addresses as well. This allows us to ban troublemakers and people who do not follow the rules. We also use Javascript for some of the sites features. The scripts are hosted on site. We DO NOT pass this info to third parties.

We allow embedding of various online services (like YouTube) on Finalchan. We cannot stop these services from collecting your information, and do not help them get the information.
